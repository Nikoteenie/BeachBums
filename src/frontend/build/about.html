The Beach Bums

Beach Bums Story

Back in Oct 2022, Three cool kids from a class In Generation USA decided to take a celebration trip over to South Beach Miami for completing their program. On the way they started listing all the things they needed when they realized none of the e-commerce solutions gave them what they needed. So they built their own! Today, beach-goers from all over the globe use our website for all their beach desires.

Our Mission

Beach Bums was designed to be your one- stop shop for everything you’ll ever need for a day at the beach! Our products were created with you in mind. Hopefully we can bring joy and fun to you and your family.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Most of our products are made of recyclable and compostable materials which mean no worries for our ocean life. If something you bought from us is left at the beach or dropped in the ocean our marina will be 100% safe! Some of our proceeds go to our Sustainability Fund which is intended to reduce waste by building products and programs to support this team and our merchants. We love the animals and the planet, you bum.
